My Travel Stories

A glimpse into my favorite destinations and experiences around the world.

Ella, Sri Lanka

Ella is a small mountain town surrounded by tea plantations and misty hills. The scenic views and relaxed atmosphere make it a must-visit for nature lovers.

Highlights:

  • Hike to Ella Rock
  • Visit Nine Arches Bridge
  • Enjoy tea tasting at a local factory

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Known as the Lion Rock, Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage site. It offers breathtaking panoramic views and fascinating historical significance.

Highlights:

  • Climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore ancient frescoes
  • Walk through royal gardens

Galle, Sri Lanka

Galle is a charming coastal city famous for its well-preserved Dutch Fort. Its cobblestone streets, ocean views, and colonial architecture create a timeless atmosphere.

Highlights:

  • Walk along Galle Fort walls
  • Explore local cafes and boutiques
  • Watch the sunset over the Indian Ocean
