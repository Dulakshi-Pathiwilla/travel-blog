Travel Destinations

Colombo Galle Kandy Trincomalee
Colombo is the Sri Lanka's bustling commercial capital, blending mdern city life with colonial heritage and vibrant coastal atmosphere. Galle, famous for its UNESCO-listed Dutch Fort, is a charming coastal city with a mix of history, cultur and scenic beaches. Kandy, nestled in the hills, is the island's cultural heart, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth relic and rich traditions. Trincomalee, located on the northeast coast, renowned for its natural harbour, pristine beaches, and spiritual sites like the Knoeshwaram Temple.
